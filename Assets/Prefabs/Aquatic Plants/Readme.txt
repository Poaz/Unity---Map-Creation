Version 1.1

A simple package of 3D Aquatic Plants. You will find:
-One Southern Cattail
-One Water Lily Flower (With FOUR different materials)
-Two Water Lilies Leaves

The Water Lily Flower have FOUR differents textures you can choose from and change you Prefab as you like. 
All you have to do is select the prefab and in the Material Component, click the box with the 
current Texture and choose another Texture from the Select Texture Window.

Then just drop the prefabs in your scene. Pretty easy!
